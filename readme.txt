Talentflow - Employee Management System with AI Features

Talentflow is an employee management system designed to streamline HR processes and provide valuable insights using artificial intelligence.

Key AI Features:
- Attrition Risk Analysis: Predict potential employee turnover.
- Employee Sentiment Analysis: Gauge employee morale and feedback.
- Generate Job Description: Automate the creation of job descriptions.
- Smart Candidate Matching: Identify the best candidates for open positions.
